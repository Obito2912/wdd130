Overview

Purpose

The purpose of our online presence is to reach more adventure enthusiasts and make it easy for them to learn about and book our rafting trips. We aim to provide essential information, showcase our services, and connect with customers for inquiries and updates. This will help us grow our community, increase bookings, and strengthen our brand.

Audience

Our target customers are adventure enthusiasts aged 18-45, with moderate to high disposable income, who enjoy outdoor activities and seek thrilling experiences.
