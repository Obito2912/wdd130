Dominican Republic

Dominican Republic is a little island located in the Caribbean and it is one of the top tourist destination. Popular destinations include Punta Cana, Puerto Plata, and Samana. Dominican culture is a mixture of the cultures of Spaniard colonists, African slaves, and Taino natives. With its population over 10 million people, its territory stretches 18,704 square miles. It's better known for its baseball players, its mixed culture, its music, food, tropical weather around the whole year, and beaches.
